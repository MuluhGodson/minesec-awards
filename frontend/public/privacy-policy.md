# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: January 1, 2026**

The Ministry of Secondary Education (MINESEC) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use the MINESEC Awards platform.

## 1. Information We Collect
We collect personal information that you provide directly to us when you register an account, submit an application, or contact support. This includes your name, email address, phone number, and any documents uploaded as part of your application.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
Your information is used exclusively to:
- Process and evaluate your MINESEC Awards application.
- Communicate with you regarding your application status.
- Improve our platform and services.

## 3. Information Sharing
We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties. Your application details will only be shared with the official MINESEC Awards Jury and authorized administrative personnel.

## 4. Data Security
We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. All sensitive data is encrypted during transmission and securely stored in our databases.

## 5. Your Rights
You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information at any time. Please contact us if you wish to exercise these rights.
